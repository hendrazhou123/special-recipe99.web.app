The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Get 1/2 cup softened butter
  2. Get 2-3 bananas
  3. Get 2 eggs
  4. Take 3/4 cup sugar
  5. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  6. Take 1 1/2 cups flour
  7. Make ready Splash vanilla extract
  8. Prepare Chocolate chips/nuts/raisins whatever your heart wants❤️

Steps to make Banana Bread:
  1. First combine - eggs, bananas, & butter
  2. Once the first step is mixed well add in baking soda, sugar, flour & extract.
  3. Fold in chocolate/nuts/raisins etc.
  4. Pour into a buttered or sprayed loaf pan. Bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 45-50 minutes.
