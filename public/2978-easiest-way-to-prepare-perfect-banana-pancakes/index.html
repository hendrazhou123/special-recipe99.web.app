The ingredients needed to make Banana Pancakes:
  1. Make ready 1 mashed banana
  2. Get 2 eggs
  3. Get 2 tbsp flour
  4. Get 1/8 tsp baking powder
  5. Take 1/8 tsp kosher salt
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
