Instructions to make Green Velvet Cake:
  1. Preheat oven 350°F
  2. In large bowl, mix butter and salt then beat with hand mixer at medium speed until creamy
  3. In a mixing add flour and baking soda. Stir well
  4. In small bowl add cocoa and coloring. Beat well.
  6. In the large bowl with butter add eggs one at a time.
  7. Then mix everything together. Mix well till creamy. Pour into cake pans. ( I used a bunt pan)
  8. Bake for 35 to 45 mins.
  9. Cool 10 mins. Turn over onto rack to cool. When cool frost.
