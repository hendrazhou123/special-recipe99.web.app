The ingredients needed to make Red Velvet Cake:
  1. Get 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. Take 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  3. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. Prepare 1 stick (4 ounces) salted butter at room temperature
  6. Take 2 cups sugar
  7. Take 3 large eggs
  8. Prepare 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  9. Make ready 1 cup sour cream
  10. Take 1/2 cup whole milk
  11. Make ready 1 1/2 tablespoons red food coloring
