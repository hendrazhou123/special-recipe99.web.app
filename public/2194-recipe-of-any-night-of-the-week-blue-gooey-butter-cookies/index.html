The ingredients needed to make Blue Gooey Butter Cookies!!!:
  1. Prepare 1 box Duncan Hines Blue Velvet Cake Mix (16.5oz)
  2. Get 1/2 cup butter
  3. Take 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Get 1 egg
  5. Make ready 1 box (8oz) cream cheese, softened
  6. Take 1 powdered sugar

Steps to make Blue Gooey Butter Cookies!!!:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix together cream cheese, vanilla, egg and butter with an electric mixer til fluffy.
  3. Add in cake mix and mix well. Be careful with this part or your kitchen will be blue! Lol it flies everywhere so start off slow!
  4. Refrigerate dough for 30-35 min til nice and cold!
  5. After dough has chilled roll into balls then roll in powdered sugar and place on a greased cookie sheet. Bake for 10-14 min.
