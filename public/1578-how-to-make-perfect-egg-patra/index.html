Steps to make Egg Patra:
  1. Take a glass vishk 2 egg add salt, turmeric, green chilli paste, garlic ginger,paste, onion,green onion, coriander and whisk again.. Add everything as per your taste take a pan add oil or butter or both on slow gas make a omelette
  2. As omlete is done grate cheese on it make a roll and cut in swizz role.
  3. Now take a pan put oil or butter add garlic ginger paste, onion and stir for min or so till the onion gets pink Add egg yolk boiled, and stir add water and let it boil for 2 mins add tomato add full gas stir till oil gets separated.
  4. Add salt turmeric powder chilli powder curry masala garam masala and fry till..
  5. Now add little water add the left over white egg in cubes.
  6. Now put the roll on the plate put the gravy over it. Sprinkle coriander over it.
  7. Serve with bread pav roti or jowar roti..
