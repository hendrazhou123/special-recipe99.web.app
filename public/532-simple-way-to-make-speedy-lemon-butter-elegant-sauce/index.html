The ingredients needed to make Lemon butter elegant sauce:
  1. Prepare 1 kg Chopped White onion
  2. Prepare 1 kg Chopped Roma Tomatoes
  3. Get 400 G Capers
  4. Prepare 1 cup chopped mix Herbs
  5. Get 2 Kg Frozen Butter
  6. Prepare Pinch kosher salt and ground black pepper
  7. Take 1 cup chopped garlic
  8. Make ready 1 cup lemon juice

Steps to make Lemon butter elegant sauce:
  1. Sauté translucent the onion garlic and tomatoes and capers in olive oil
  2. When everything’s got pale,, pour in the chunks of frozen butter to the mixture, in a low heat, preventing the butter to gets clarify.
  3. Stir it continuously until all the butter dissolve and incorporate to all vegetable mixture, and only having a fine smooth thick butter sauce. Take off to the heat then season with salt and pepper, adjust the flavour by adding lemon juice.
