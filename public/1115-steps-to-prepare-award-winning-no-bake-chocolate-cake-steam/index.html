The ingredients needed to make No Bake Chocolate Cake (Steam):
  1. Get 1 cup Flour
  2. Prepare 1/3 Cocoa Powder
  3. Make ready 1 tsp Baking Powder & Soda
  4. Prepare I cup Sugar
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp Salt
  6. Prepare 2 Large Eggs
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup Vegetable Oil
  8. Take 1/2 Cup Milk
  9. Take 1/4 tsp Vanilla Essence
  10. Take 1/4 tsp Instant Coffee

Steps to make No Bake Chocolate Cake (Steam):
  1. Sift the Flour, Cocoa Powder and Baking Powder and Baking Soda into a large bowl
  2. In the same bowl, add the sugar and Salt. Mix
  3. Add the Oil, Milk and Vanilla Essence and Coffee
  4. Whisk
  5. Steam for 40 mins. Medium Heat
  6. Add any toppings you want (Icing Sugar, Oreo, Choc Syrup)
  7. Enjoy!
