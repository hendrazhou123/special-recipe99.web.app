The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Sammy:
  1. Make ready 5 ounces (1 1/4 cups) whole-wheat pastry flour
  2. Prepare 1 teaspoon baking soda
  3. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon salt
  4. Prepare 4 ounces (1 stick) unsalted butter, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  5. Get Heaped 1/2 cup smooth organic peanut butter, with no salt or sugar added, plus 2 tablespoons for filling the cookies
  6. Make ready 5 ounces (3/4 cup) raw brown sugar
  7. Take 1 egg
