The ingredients needed to make Okara Lemon Cookies Made with Vegetable Oil:
  1. Get 1 Egg yolk (room temperature)
  2. Take 1 just 1 dash Natural Salt
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  4. Get 50 ml Vegetable oil
  5. Make ready 40 grams Powdered sugar
  6. Get 70 grams ○Cake flour
  7. Make ready 30 grams ○ Dried okara (store-bought)
  8. Get 20 grams ○Corn starch

Instructions to make Okara Lemon Cookies Made with Vegetable Oil:
  1. Combine and sift the cake flour and the corn starch.
  2. Add the lemon juice and salt to the egg and blend with an electric mixer.
  3. When it's well mixed, add the vegetable oil little by little, blending with each addition, until it thickens.
  4. Add the powdered sugar and blend some more.
  5. Combine and fold in the well-sifted ingredients from step one and the okara.
  6. When the dough has come together, wrap and let it sit in the fridge for over 30 minutes.
  7. Preheat the oven to 170°C. Roll out the dough and cut out any shapes you like.
  8. Bake at 170°C for 13-15 minutes.
