The ingredients needed to make Roasted Peanut Stew:
  1. Get 1 cup unpeeled roasted red peanuts
  2. Prepare 1 bunch Italian parsley
  3. Get 1 bulb fennel
  4. Prepare 1 bunch garlic chives
  5. Prepare 1 large onion
  6. Take 5 local garlic sections
  7. Make ready 3 very ripe tomatoes
  8. Get 6 pickled green olives
  9. Make ready 6 pickled black olives
  10. Take 15 pickled capers
  11. Make ready To taste salt (careful, the pickles are very salty!)
  12. Get 3 tablespoons vegetable cooking oil

Instructions to make Roasted Peanut Stew:
  1. Chop the onion, fennel, garlic chives and Italian parsley and suet in the oil.
  2. Chop the tomatoes and add them together with the peanuts. Remember, don't peel the peanuts! Deglace with a little water and let to cook until tomatoes break down.
  3. Chop the garlic and pickles and add them to the stew. Add water but only a little. The stew shouldn't have a lot of liquid.
  4. Cook until having a thick consistency, around 8 minutes, and take it out of the heat.
  5. Taste and correct seasoning.
  6. Serve with quinoa rice.
