The ingredients needed to make Lemon Sugar Cookies:
  1. Prepare 1 stick butter, softened
  2. Take 1 1/4 cup flour
  3. Prepare 1 cup white sugar, separated
  4. Take 1 tbsp brown sugar
  5. Prepare 2 tsp vanilla
  6. Make ready 1 egg yolk
  7. Make ready Zest of 2 small/medium lemons
  8. Take 1/4 tsp baking soda
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  10. Get 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions to make Lemon Sugar Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. In a small bowl, combine flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Whisk together to combine.
  3. In a medium bowl, using a hand or standing mixer, cream the butter, brown sugar and 3/4c sugar until light and fluffy. (about 3-4 minutes or so). Add egg, lemon zest and vanilla and beat until combined.
  4. Slowly pour in the dry ingredients and beat just until combined–do not over mix. Roll cookie dough into about 1 1/2 inch balls and roll into the remaining 1/4 cup sugar (add sugar into a small bowl first.) and place about 2 inches apart on a parchment lined baking pan.
  5. Bake 9-11 minutes. Allow to cool before eating.
  6. Store in an air tight container or freeze. (They never last long in my glass cookie jar lol!)
