The ingredients needed to make Red velvet and chocolate sponge cake:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cup flour
  2. Get 1 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 7 medium size eggs

  2. Separate egg whites and egg yolks. Add the egg whites to a large bowl and mix incorporating with your sugar till stiff peak. Add vanilla to your egg yolks and start adding it to the egg whites one at a time then turn off your mixer.
