Instructions to make Tripolitan_meat_pie:
  1. Dough preparation: - - In a suitable bowl, sift the flour. Add sugar, salt and mix. Add the egg, oil and water as needed. Knead until a soft / smooth dough is obtained - Cut the dough into balls (size as desired)
  2. Preparation of meat filling: In a saucepan, add onions, meat, salt and stir until the liquids evaporate. Remove from heat and allow to cool
  3. Next, mix in the tahini, pomegranate molasses, mint, vinegar, parsley, spices and adjust the salt if needed
  4. To prepare the meat pie: - - On a wide wooden board, or a bench top, with enough room to stretch the dough
  5. Using your hands coat all the dough balls with cornstarch
  6. Unused dough pieces are placed on a cotton towel and covered to prevent from drying until ready for use
  7. Next, mix the oil and butter together - rub oil on the dough by hand, making sure to cover all over, then stretch out the dough until a very thin layer is established
  8. Cut the dough edges off to make sure only thin layers are used. Continue to stretch by hand, as much as possible until it becomes very thin
  9. Roll the dough in a square or circular shape
  10. Dough pies are placed on an oven tray, press down on them a little
  11. Begin to distribute the meat filling on the stretched dough, using a suitable amount
  12. Bake in a pre-heated oven at 200 ° C or 400 ° F for 12-18 minutes or until crispy and golden
  13. Served hot with fresh mint leaves
