Steps to make Milk Chocolate Caramel Swirl Bark:
  1. Melt chocolate in the microwave until smooth. Then melt caramel with cream until smooth.
  2. Pour chocolate onto parchment paper and smooth out. Let chocolate begin to harden but not set (5-10 minutes). Do not allow to set. Add dots of caramel sauce to the chocolate and with a knife gently swirl the caramel through the chocolate. Sprinkle the top with toffee chips and add a light sprinkle of kosher salt.
