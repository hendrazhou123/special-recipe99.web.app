Instructions to make Zero oil mix vegetable pasanda:
  1. Heat a wok and add milk and onion in it.
  2. Now add grated cauliflower green chilli and garlic cloves.
  3. Add Cinnamon stick and clove and mix it well. Simmer it until Onion becomes soft.
  4. When milk is nearly evaporated turn off the flame and let it cool completely.
  5. Now add this in blending jar and blend it. Your Onion paste is ready.Keep it aside.
  6. Now take a bowl and add milk curd and besan in it. Mix it nicely to avoid lumps.
  7. Now again heat a wok and add cumin seeds and dry roast them for few seconds.
  8. Now add Onion paste and mix it for 2 to 3 minutes.
  9. Now add curd besan mixture and cook for another minutes.
  10. Now add water in it and mix it well.
  11. Now add salt as per your taste.
  12. Also add garam masala and mix everything properly.
  13. Now add all the boiled vegetables and mix it well. Cook it for 5 minutes.
  14. After 5 minutes turn off the flame. Your yummy and healthy zero oil mix vegetable pasanda is ready to serve hot.
