The ingredients needed to make Garlic Butter Bread With Sweet Corn Dip:
  1. Make ready 2 slices bread
  2. Get 1/2 tbsp unsalted butter (melted)
  3. Take 2 garlic (grated)
  4. Get 1 tsp dried oregano
  5. Take 1 pack instant corn soup
  6. Prepare 1 tsp starch flour
  7. Make ready Water
