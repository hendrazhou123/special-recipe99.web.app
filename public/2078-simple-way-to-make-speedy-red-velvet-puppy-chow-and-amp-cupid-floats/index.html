The ingredients needed to make Red Velvet Puppy Chow & Cupid Floats:
  1. Prepare RED VELVET PUPPY CHOW…
  2. Prepare 5 cups Rice Chex Cereal
  3. Prepare 1 cup red velvet cake mix
  4. Get 2 oz cream cheese
  5. Get 1 1/2 tbsp milk
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  7. Get 3/4 cup white choc. chips
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup choc. chips
  9. Prepare 1 cup valentine's colored m&ms
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup valentine's sprinkles
  11. Make ready CUPID FLOATS…
  12. Get 2 liter cherry 7up
  13. Take vanilla ice cream
  14. Get red licorice

Steps to make Red Velvet Puppy Chow & Cupid Floats:
  1. Pour chex into large mixing bowl.
  2. In seperate bowl melt choc. Chips in microwave or on stove (your choice). Careful not to burn. Add cream cheese & milk to melted chips & beat until smooth. Pour melted cream cheese & chips to bowl with chex. Mix until chex is completely coated.
  3. In a 17 gallon ziploc bag pour powdered sugar & red velvet cake mix.
  4. Add coated chex mix to powdered sugar cake mix, close & shake shake shake!!!
  5. Pour chex mix from the bag on cookie sheet to cool. When cooled put in a serving bowl & decorate with m&ms & sprinkles.
