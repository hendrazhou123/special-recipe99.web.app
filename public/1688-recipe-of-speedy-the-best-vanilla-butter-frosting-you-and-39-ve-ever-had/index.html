The ingredients needed to make The Best Vanilla Butter Frosting You've Ever Had:
  1. Prepare 5 tbsp flour
  2. Make ready 1 cup whole milk
  3. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla
  4. Make ready 1 cup granulated sugar
  5. Take 1 cup butter (room temperature)

Steps to make The Best Vanilla Butter Frosting You've Ever Had:
  1. Combine milk and flour in a sauce pan. Whisk them together until well combined. Heat until the mixture thickens stirring continuously and it will be thick, thick, thick.
  2. Let the frosting cool completely. This is important! If you are in a hurry, place the sauce pan over a bed of ice in the sink (about 10 minutes).
  3. Once the flour/milk mixture has cooled add the vanilla and stir well.
  4. Using a mixer, beat the sugar and butter together. I scrape the side of the bowl several times and beat some more (about two times).
  5. Now add the flour/milk/vanilla mixture to the mixing bowl. Beat the crap out of it until it resembles whipped cream. You don't want any sugar graininess in the frosting.
