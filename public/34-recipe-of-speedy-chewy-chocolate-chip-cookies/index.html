The ingredients needed to make Chewy chocolate chip cookies:
  1. Prepare 1.5 cups flour
  2. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup soft butter
  4. Make ready 2/3 cup light brown sugar
  5. Get 1/4 cup white sugar
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp vanilla
  7. Get 2 eggs
  8. Prepare Tons of chocolate chips!

Instructions to make Chewy chocolate chip cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees
  2. Cream warm butter. Add sugars. Then add vanilla and egg.
  3. Mix flour and baking powder in separate bowl then add slowly to wet mixture.
  4. Stir chocolate chips with spoon.
  5. Scoop tablespoons onto nonstick cookie sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes. Cool before eating.
