The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Prepare 2 1/4 cups Flour
  2. Make ready 1 tsp Baking Soda
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp Salt
  4. Prepare 1 cup Butter
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup Granulated Sugar
  6. Get 3/4 cup Light Brown Sugar
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  8. Prepare 2 Large Eggs
  9. Make ready 2 cups Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips

Steps to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Heat oven to 375¬∞F.
  2. Stir flour, baking soda, and salt together.
  3. Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla in a large bowl until creamy.
  4. Add eggs to the sugar mixture and beat well.
  5. Add the sugar mixture to the flour mixture and blend until smooth.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Use 1/2 Tb measuring spoon rounded to drop cookies onto lined baking sheet.
  8. Bake 8-10 minutes or until lightly browned. Remove onto wire rack and enjoy!
