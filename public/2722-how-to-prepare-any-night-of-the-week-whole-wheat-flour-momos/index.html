The ingredients needed to make Whole wheat flour momos:
  1. Make ready 2 cup whole wheat flour
  2. Make ready 1 cup maida/All-purpose flour
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon oil
  4. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  5. Make ready Stuffing
  6. Take 1 cup grated cabbage
  7. Get 1/2 cup grated carrot
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup paneer

  10. Get 1 teaspoon salt

  12. Make ready Schezwan sauce/momos chutney
  13. Make ready 8-10 red dried chilli
  14. Make ready 2 tomatoes
  15. Make ready 4 garlic cloves
  16. Get 1/2 tsp sugar
  17. Prepare to taste salt

Instructions to make Whole wheat flour momos:
  1. Take all the ingredients of dough and make dough…not so stiff…not so loose.cover it..keep aside for about 15-20 min
  2. Meanwhile prepare stuffing
  3. Take oil in pan…saute onion…and then add all the vegetables…add salt and black pepper..saute a little
  4. Don't fry too much….just have. To remove the raw veggie taste and water in the veggies
  5. Now take dough…make very small balls.
  6. Roll it..fill it with stuffing.
  7. Seal it…like we make plates of saree….then join the end with help of water
  8. Boil the water….keep a steel sieve over it…if you don't have steamer….
  9. Brush the sieve with oil….keep momos on it to steam…do not overload it
  10. Steam for around 10-12 min. Do not over steam
  11. Sprinkle oregano and maggi masala
  12. Serve it with chutney….
  13. To prepare momos chutney…boil red chillis and potatoes for around 10 min
  14. Sieve the water…peel off tomato…ground them all to make a paste
  15. Then turn of tadka…take oil in pan..add garlic, saute a little,add chutney….sugar, salt, vinegar.
  16. Boil it..chutney is ready.let it cool.
