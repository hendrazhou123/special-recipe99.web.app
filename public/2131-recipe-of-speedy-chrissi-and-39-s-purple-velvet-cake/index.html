The ingredients needed to make Chrissi's Purple Velvet Cake:
  1. Take 2 Eggs
  2. Get 1 cup Sugar
  3. Get 2 tbsp Cocoa Powder
  4. Get 1/2 oz Violet Icing Color
  5. Prepare 2 stick of Butter
  6. Make ready 2 1/2 cup Cake Flour
  7. Make ready 1 tsp Salt
  8. Make ready 1 cup Buttermilk
  9. Make ready 1 tsp Vanilla
  10. Take 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp White Vinegar
  12. Get Cream Cheese Icing
  13. Take 2 cup Powdered Sugar
  14. Get 8 oz Cream Cheese
  15. Take 1/2 stick of Butter
  16. Prepare 1 tbsp Vanilla
  17. Get 1 Splash of Almond Extract

Steps to make Chrissi's Purple Velvet Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. Beat eggs and add in sugar
  3. Mix in cocoa powder and icing color. *Make sure that the icing color is a concentrated paste.
  4. Add butter.
  5. Sift in cake flour and salt. Mix well.
  6. Mix in buttermilk
  7. Add vanilla
  8. Mix vinegar and baking soda in a small bowl. Add the mixture into batter.
  9. Pour into a greased bundt pan.
  10. Bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Allow to cool for an hour.
  12. ICING: Mix all ingredients together.
  13. Ice the cake and serve.
