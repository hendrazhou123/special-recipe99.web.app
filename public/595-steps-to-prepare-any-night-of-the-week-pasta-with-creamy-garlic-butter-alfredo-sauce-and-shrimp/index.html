Steps to make Pasta with Creamy Garlic Butter Alfredo Sauce and Shrimp:
  1. Cook pasta according to directions
  2. While pasta is cooking make the sauce
  3. Melt a stick of butter in saucepan over medium heat. Add 1/4 cup crushed garlic. Cook stirring. It should simmer 2 minutes
  4. Add 4 TBS flour slowly whisking briskly to avoid lumps. Slowly whisk in 1 and a half cups of milk. Bring to simmer and cook stirring until thickened.
  5. Add 2 TBS butter to large skillet. Add 1 TBS crushed garlic.
  6. When butter is melted add approx 3 cups shrimp (30-40)
  7. Fold and stir until shrimp is coated with garlic butter. Put lid on and simmer on low for 5 minutes
  8. Add sauce mixture to shrimp. Stirring until blended
  9. Drain pasta
  10. Pour shrimp and sauce over pasta
  11. Mix and enjoy
