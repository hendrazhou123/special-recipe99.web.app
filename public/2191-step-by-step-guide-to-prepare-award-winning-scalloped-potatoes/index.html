The ingredients needed to make Scalloped Potatoes:
  1. Get 5 medium Potatoes, boiled and sliced
  2. Prepare 1/3 medium onion, chopped
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp butter
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup milk
  5. Get 1/3 cup sour cream
  6. Make ready 1/3 cup velvet cheese
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp flour
  8. Take 1/2 packages cream cheese
  9. Make ready 1 cup shredded cheese
  10. Make ready salt
  11. Make ready black pepper
  12. Make ready garlic powder

Steps to make Scalloped Potatoes:
  1. Boil potatoes, let cool peel or not and slice as desired a separate bowl.
  2. Add chopped onions and 1tbsp butter in a bowl and toss.
  3. In a saucepan blend velvet, sour cream, milk, flour and cream cheese…salt, garlic powder and pepper as needed.
  4. Pour 1/2 of the cheese mixture over potatoes and onions and some of the shredded cheese and toss.
  5. Place everything in baking dish of choice and cover with shredded cheese and top with other 2 tbsp of butter bake for 20 to 30 min or until desired potatoe texture required…you can cover with foil while baking if desired.
