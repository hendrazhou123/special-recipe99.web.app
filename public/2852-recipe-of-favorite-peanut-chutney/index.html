The ingredients needed to make Peanut Chutney:
  1. Get 1 cup Peanut
  2. Get 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  3. Take 1 Chopped onion
  4. Prepare 3 pods Garlic
  5. Prepare 3 Red chilies
  6. Prepare piece Tamarind small
  7. Take 2 tbsp Oil
  8. Prepare to taste Salt
  9. Prepare To Tamper-
  10. Get 1 tsp Mustard seeds
  11. Make ready 1 tsp Urad dal
  12. Prepare 1 sprig Curry leaves
  13. Take 2 Red chillies

Steps to make Peanut Chutney:
  1. Heat oil in a pan and dry roast the peanuts till it turned slightly browned.
  2. In the same pan heat some oil and fry the cumin seeds, chopped onions, salt, red chilies, garlic pods and tamarind.
  3. Let them cool completely with the Peanuts
  4. Once everything cooled completely, grind them into a smooth paste by adding little water
  5. In the same pan heat some oil and add the items under tamper and add it to the chutney
  6. Peanut chutney is ready to serve
