The ingredients needed to make Garlic and Herb butter crusted Tri Tip Roast:
  1. Take 2-3 Lb Tri Tip Roast
  2. Make ready For Garlic Butter: ⬇️
  3. Prepare 2 Garlic Cloves
  4. Make ready 12 oz Salted or Unsalted butter, room temp
  5. Take 3 sprig fresh rosemary
  6. Make ready 4 sprig fresh Thyme
  7. Make ready 1 TBS oregano
  8. Get 1 TBS parsley
  9. Prepare 2.5 Teaspoon Lemon pepper, AND white pepper
  10. Take 2 Teaspoon Dijon grain mustard
  11. Make ready 1 TBS Worcheshire sauce
  12. Get 1/2 TBS paprika
  13. Make ready 1/2 TBS chopped dried onion, AND onion powder
  14. Get 1/2 TBS Montreal Steak seasoning

Steps to make Garlic and Herb butter crusted Tri Tip Roast:
  1. Rub Montreal steak seasoning on Tri Tip Roast, let come to room temp
  2. Combine all ingredients for Garlic and herb butter
  3. Rub Garlic butter on room temp sirloin roast
  4. Preheat Oven to 425*, put Roast in roasting pan or casserole dish, Roast 15-20 minutes per LB (about 30 minutes for Med Rare, 45 for medium) let rest 10-15 minutes
  5. Serve with potato, gravy and vegetable!
