Instructions to make Turkey Meatballs in Thai Red Coconut Curry:
  1. Mix all the turkey meatball ingredients. Stir well.
  2. Shape balls using a medium ice cream spoon. Arrange on a cookie sheet that has been covered with parchment paper.
  3. Bake in preheated 200C / 400F oven for 25-30 minutes. - Remove from the oven. Set aside.
  4. Heat the oil over medium high heat.Saute the onions until fragrant.
  5. Add the red and orange peppers. Cook until wilted.
  6. Add water, red curry paste, and coconut milk while stirring continually until it boils.
  7. Add fish sauce and mushroom bouillon. Stir well. Adjust to taste.
  8. Add in the turkey balls. Cook again to a boil. Remove from the heat.
