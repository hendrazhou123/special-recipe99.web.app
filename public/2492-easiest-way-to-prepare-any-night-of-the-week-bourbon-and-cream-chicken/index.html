The ingredients needed to make Bourbon and Cream Chicken:
  1. Take 2-3 lbs chicken breast
  2. Make ready Olive oil
  3. Take 3 tbsp butter
  4. Get 2.5 cups mushrooms
  5. Take 2 cups spinach
  6. Take 1 pint heavy cream
  7. Make ready 2 shallots
  8. Take 2 tbsp fresh thyme
  9. Get 1/3 cup flour
  10. Prepare 1/3 Kentucky bourbon
  11. Prepare Salt
  12. Take Pepper

Instructions to make Bourbon and Cream Chicken:
  1. Cut chicken into strips. Coat with flour, salt, and pepper in a bag. Shake.
  2. Pour into skillet with oil. Cook on med-low temp until done. Chicken should be tender, not hard. Remove and set aside
  3. In same skillet, add butter and shallots. Sautee 1 minute. Add mushrooms. Sautee for another minute
  4. Add bourbon. Dont freak out if it flames up. This happens with strong burbon
  5. Add the rest of the ingredients, starting with the cream. This includes the cooked chicken
  6. Simmer on low for about 10 minutes. Add salt and pepper if desired. I like the cream saltier
