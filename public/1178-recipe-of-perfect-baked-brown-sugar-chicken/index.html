The ingredients needed to make Baked Brown Sugar Chicken:
  1. Get 4 boneless skinless chicken breast
  2. Get 4 cloves garlic minced
  3. Prepare 3 Tbsp olive oil
  4. Take 4 Tbsp brown sugar

Steps to make Baked Brown Sugar Chicken:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°. Place chicken in a baking pan.
  2. In a small pan, cook garlic in oil for 2-3 minutes, remove from heat and stir in brown sugar.
  3. Spread over chicken and bake for 45 minutes or untill done. Serve and enjoy.
