The ingredients needed to make delicious red velvet cupcakes topped with creamy cream cheese:
  1. Prepare base
  2. Prepare 2 1/2 cup cake flour
  3. Get 2 tbsp unsweetened dutch cocoa powder
  4. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 cup sugar
  6. Get 1 cup canola oil
  7. Make ready 2 large eggs
  8. Get 1/2 tsp red colouring
  9. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  10. Take 1 cup plain yoghurt
  11. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  12. Make ready 2 tsp white vinegar
  13. Make ready frosting
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  15. Take 1 packages philadelphia cream cheese
  16. Get 1 cup icing sugar
  17. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps to make delicious red velvet cupcakes topped with creamy cream cheese:
  1. preheat oven 350°F
  2. whisk together cake flour,cocoa and salt.
  3. in another bowl, whisk together oil and sugar until combined then add eggs one at a time. mix in food colour and vanilla.
  4. add flour and yoghurt and whisk well. stir together the baking soda and vinegar in small bowl, then add it into the batter, beat for 10 seconds.
  5. divide batter evenly in cups and bake for 15 minutes depend on the size of the cups.
  6. let the cupcakes cool before putting on the frosting.
  7. for the frosting, beat one whole packet of cream cheese, then add butter. do not overbeat.
  8. after the mixture is combined, add vanilla extract.
  9. add icing sugar until the texture is thick enough to hold the shape.
  10. put the frosting in the fridge for 15 mins before decorate the cupcakes
