The ingredients needed to make Red Velvet Mug Cake:
  1. Prepare 4 tsp All purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 tbsp Unsweetened cocoa powder
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp Baking powder
  4. Get 1/8 tsp Salt
  5. Get 2 tsp Granulated sugar
  6. Take 3 tbsp Buttermilk
  7. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp Oil
  8. Make ready 1/8 tsp Red colour
  9. Get 2 tbsp White choco chips

Steps to make Red Velvet Mug Cake:
  1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Pour the ready batter in a mug, add choco chips and mix well. Bake it in micro at high power for 1 & 1/2 mins.
  3. Remove from microwave and enjoy delicious Red Velvet Mug Cake.
