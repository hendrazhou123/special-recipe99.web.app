Instructions to make Pumpkin Swirl ðŸŽƒ Cake Brownies:
  1. Prepare brownie mix as directed on box, then mix in your chocolate morsels, set mixture off to the side…..
  2. In a separate bowl with an electric mixer beat together your pumpkin, cream cheese, sugar, pumpkin pie spice and vanilla extract, set bowl off to the side…..
  3. Spray a 7 X 11 inch glass baking dish with the butter cooking spray…..
  4. Spoon your brownie mix into your greased baking dish……
  5. Now drop by spoonfuls your pumpkin mix into the brownie mixture…….
  6. Take a dull knife and carefully swirl pumpkin mix into the brownie mix…..
  7. Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 30 - 40 minutes, or until tooth pick inserted in center comes out clean…..
  8. Let brownies rest for 20 minutes…..
