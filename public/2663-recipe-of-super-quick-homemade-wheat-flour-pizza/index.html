Steps to make Wheat flour pizza:
  1. Take a big bowl,mix wheat flour, yougurt,baking powder,baking soda, salt and Knead dough and keep aside for 1 hour
  2. In a pan heat 1 teaspoon oil or ghee as per your taste. I used ghee.
  3. Add onion to the pan fry for a min add ginger garlic paste 1/2 teaspoon
  4. Now one by one add all veggies to the pan you can add pizza sauce or masala to it but Lock down effect not getting everything.
  5. Saute all veggies for a minute.do not cook them.add salt,and some herbs to it.
  6. After one hour, take the dough.punch it for a while.again need for minute gently.
  7. Roll out the pizza as per your choice,i made it thin as my child prefers that
  8. With help of fork mark it so that it won't rise much
  9. Now apply Ginger garlic paste on it, apply some tomato sauce, put all the veggies on it add grated paneer and cheese and sprinkle herbs on it.
  10. Bake it in preheated pan for 8 to 10 mins.serve hot.
