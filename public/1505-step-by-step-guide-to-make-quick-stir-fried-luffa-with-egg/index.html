Steps to make 丝瓜炒蛋 Stir-fried Luffa with Egg:
  1. 切除丝瓜的外皮和种子 Peel the luffa, and remove the seeds
  2. 把丝瓜切细条 Cut luffa into thin slices
  3. 热油爆香一汤匙蒜米 Heat the oil with 1 tablespoon of minced garlic
  4. 加入丝瓜翻炒 Add luffa and stir-fry
  5. 加入鸡蛋，一汤匙酱清。翻炒，盖锅焖3分钟 Add the eggs, 1 tablespoon of light soy sauce. Stir-fry and simmer with lid for 3mins
