The ingredients needed to make Egg manchurian:
  1. Take 4 eggs
  2. Get 1/2 tsp curry powder
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp paprika
  4. Get 1/2 tsp garam masala
  5. Take Cooking oil
  6. Make ready Salt
  7. Get Spring onions
  8. Take 2 tomatoes(ripe and peeled)
  9. Make ready 2 cloves garlic
  10. Make ready Little water for paste
  11. Make ready 100 g all purpose flour
  12. Prepare 1 corriander(Dania)

Instructions to make Egg manchurian:
