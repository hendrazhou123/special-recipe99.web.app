The ingredients needed to make Turmeric milk:
  1. Make ready 2 teaspoon turmeric
  2. Get 1 teaspoon desi ghee
  3. Prepare 4 cup milk
  4. Take 1 cup water
  5. Take 3-4 cardamom
  6. Get 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  7. Make ready 2-3 laung or cloves
  8. Make ready 1 teaspoon ajwain or carom seeds
  9. Take 1 big Elaichi or cardamom black
  10. Get Sugar according to taste 3 teaspoons
