The ingredients needed to make Soba Noodles With Fried Tofu:
  1. Take 200 grams soba noodles, cooked and drained
  2. Prepare 300 grams firm tofu
  3. Make ready 200 grams oyster mushrooms
  4. Get 1 carrot
  5. Take 3 shallots
  6. Make ready 4 tbsp soy sauce
  7. Prepare 4 tbsp mirin
  8. Prepare 4 tbsp miso soup paste
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp flour, to dust
  10. Get 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Instructions to make Soba Noodles With Fried Tofu:
  1. Heat oil in a frying pan to a medium/hot heat
  2. Cut tofu in to 1 inch cubes and dust with flour
  3. Fry tofu on all sides until golden. Leave to drain on paper towels
  4. Finely slice shallots and carrot
  5. Divide noodles, shallots, carrots, mushrooms and tofu into four bowls
  6. Add the miso soup paste to 1 litre of boiling water and stir to dissolve. Then add soy sauce and mirin.
  7. Pour hot liquid into each bowl and serve immediately
