The ingredients needed to make Butterscotch Spice Cake with Cream Cheese Frosting:
  1. Make ready 1 box spice cake
  2. Make ready 4 eggs
  3. Make ready 1-3.4 oz box butterscotch instant pudding
  4. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Get 1 cup water
  6. Make ready 1/2 vegetable oil
  7. Get 1/4 cup mayo
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup sour cream
  9. Prepare For the Frosting
  10. Get 2 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  11. Get 1/4 cup butter, softened
  12. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  13. Prepare 8 oz cream cheese, softened

Steps to make Butterscotch Spice Cake with Cream Cheese Frosting:
  1. Preheat oven 350. Grease pan you are going to cook cake in, I use a bundt pan and Crisco oil. Set pan aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl add all the ingredients for baking the cake and mix till smooth and no lumps.
  3. Pour in bundt pan and place in over for 50-60 minutes. Check with tooth pick at 50 minutes in middle comes out clean. Let Cool
  4. Making the frosting. In a large mixing bowl add powdered sugar, butter, cream cheese, & vanilla. Mix till smooth.
  5. Once cake has cooled, place on cake dish and icing the cake. If there is extra frosting left, I put in the middle of the bundt cake filling up the hole! Sprinkle some cinnamon all over the cake once you have finished putting on the frosting. Enjoy…
