The ingredients needed to make Bacon & Cheese Loaf (Cuisinart Bread Maker):
  1. Get 1 cup + 2 TBSP warm water
  2. Get 1/2 TSP salt
  3. Make ready 3 cups flour
  4. Get 2 TBSP sugar
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup cheddar
  6. Get 1/2 cup Colby Jack
  7. Prepare 1/4 Asiago Cheese
  8. Get 8 slice bacon, diced
  9. Prepare 3 TSP yeast
