Steps to make Ice cream cone cupcakes:
  1. Make cake mix
  2. Fill ice cream cones with cake mix place on cupcake pan (you will get 24 cupcakes if you fill them just right, see pictures)
  3. Bake until done (follow directions on box for cupcakes) I just keep an eye on them.
  4. Take A toothpick and put a hole in the bottom of the ice cream come with the toothpick this helps the steam to release when cooling so the ice cream cone does not get soggy place on wire rack to cool completely
  5. Ice cupcakes (can pipe on to make it look nice and neat) add any toppings
  6. I just use a cake knife I like that rough look.
  7. Notes- use less icing if you don’t want your kids to have much… the first bite is almost nothing but icing.
