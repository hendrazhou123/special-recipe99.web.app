The ingredients needed to make Bacon Wrapped Stuffed Cream Cheese Beef Poblano Pepper:
  1. Get 4 poblano peppers (pilled and deseed)
  2. Take 12 slices thin brisket
  3. Take 8 slices thin bacon
  4. Take barbecue sauce (my favorite is Head County Brand)
  5. Prepare 1 jalapeno pepper (remove seeds or leave seeds)
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup cilantro leaves
  7. Prepare 6 oz cream cheese

Steps to make Bacon Wrapped Stuffed Cream Cheese Beef Poblano Pepper:
  1. In a small food processor blend cream cheese, jalapeño pepper, and cilantro into a paste.
  2. Take 3 slices of brisket and lay next to each. Then place 1/4 of paste in center and roll meat to place in pepper's. Drizzle barbecue sauce over the meat.
  3. Take and wrap 2 slices of bacon around the peppers and drizzle barbecue sauce over the bacon.
  4. Place in oven 350 degrees for 20 minutes and then turn oven to broil last 5 minutes to brown bacon.
