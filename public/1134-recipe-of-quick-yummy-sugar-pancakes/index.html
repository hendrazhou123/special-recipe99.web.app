The ingredients needed to make Yummy sugar pancakes:
  1. Make ready 1/3 cup water
  2. Make ready Hole wheat flour
  3. Get Corn flour
  4. Prepare 1 tsp instant Yeast
  5. Prepare Black seeds if you want
  6. Prepare Sugar
  7. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  8. Make ready 2 tsp milk powder

Steps to make Yummy sugar pancakes:
  1. Put all the ingredients except the water and black seeds if you want black seeds
  2. Then mix it and while mixing it put the 1/3 water cup
  3. If the doughs soft sprinkle some black seeds if you want black seeds
  4. Then cover it for 1 hour until it rises
  5. Put the flour on a tray then separate them in balls
  6. Then tap it with a spoon to make a flat platform
  7. Put it on a pan with high heated oil like until at a temperature of boiling on the fire and be careful for children and your self so when frying be careful also splash some oil on the top of the dough with a spoon
  8. To know when it's done make sure it's has a brownish golden look on both sides
  9. If it has take it out of the fire with a fork
  10. When you done sprinkle sugar on it
