Instructions to make Cajun Chicken over Pasta:
  1. In a small bowl, combine all ingredients for the spice mixture; then coat chicken in spice mixture.
  2. In a medium, non-stick skillet sprayed with vegetable spray or covered with oil, saute chicken on medium heat until cooked through (no longer pink). Set chicken aside.
  3. In the same skillet, heat oil; saute garlic, onions and peppers for 5 minutes or until tender. Add tomatoes, basil, oregano and cayenne. Simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. Prepare pasta of choice and top with chicken. Combine with sauce.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
