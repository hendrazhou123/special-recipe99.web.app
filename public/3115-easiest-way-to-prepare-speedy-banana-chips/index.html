The ingredients needed to make Banana Chips:
  1. Make ready 8 Raw Bananas peeled
  2. Get to taste Salt
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp Red chilli powder
  4. Get as needed Oil for frying

Instructions to make Banana Chips:
  1. Take bananas and slice them with the help of slicer
  2. Next heat pan add oil for frying once heated immediately add slices and fry till it becomes crisp & golden brown
  3. Next in a mixing bowl add salt & red chilli powder put chips & mix well
  4. Now cool it & store in airtight container & enjoy whenever you want.
  5. Note:- Fry chips in batches to avoid soggy chips
