The ingredients needed to make Butter cake:
  1. Prepare 5 eggs
  2. Make ready 160-170 g Caster sugar
  3. Get 1 tbs vanilla essence
  4. Make ready 1 tbs ovalette/ cake emulsifier
  5. Get 220 g cake flour
  6. Prepare 60 ml full cream milk
  7. Get 150 g melted butter
