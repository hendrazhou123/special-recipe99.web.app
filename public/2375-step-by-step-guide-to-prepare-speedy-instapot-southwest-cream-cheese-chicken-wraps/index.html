The ingredients needed to make Instapot Southwest Cream Cheese Chicken Wraps:
  1. Prepare 3 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  2. Take 1 packet taco seasoning
  3. Make ready 1 jalapeno, diced
  4. Take 1 small onion, chopped
  5. Take 1/4 cup green onion, sliced
  6. Make ready 1 can corn, drained
  7. Make ready 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  8. Make ready 1 can Rotel, drained
  9. Make ready 1 cup sour cream
  10. Prepare 1 (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese, cubed
  11. Make ready 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  12. Take 1 lime
  13. Get 6-8 tortillas

Instructions to make Instapot Southwest Cream Cheese Chicken Wraps:
  1. Place chicken in instapot and add just enough water to coat bottom of pot (about 1/2 cup). Add onions and jalapeno and taco seasoning. Set instapot to cook for 20 minutes.
  2. Pull out cooked chicken and shred. Drain off any excess juice. Return to pot.
  3. Add all remaining ingredients including the juice from one lime. Cook an additional 5 minutes.
  4. Stir well to incorporate all ingredients. Place onto tortillas and enjoy warm or cold.
  5. Enjoy!
