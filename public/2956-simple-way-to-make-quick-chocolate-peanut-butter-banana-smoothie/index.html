The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie:
  1. Prepare 1 Banana
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 cup Almond Milk, vanilla, unsweetened
  3. Prepare 2 tablespoons Peanut Butter
  4. Get 1 tablespoon Cacao Powder
  5. Take 1/4 cup Ice
