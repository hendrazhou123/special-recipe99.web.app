The ingredients needed to make German potatoes salad:
  1. Take 4 large bakers potatoes
  2. Prepare 12 eggs
  3. Prepare 1 small diced onion
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup vinegar
  5. Get 3 cups vegetable oil
  6. Make ready 1 tsp salt (to taste)
  7. Prepare 1 Tbls pepper

Steps to make German potatoes salad:
  1. Boil potatoes 40 minutes
  2. Boil eggs 20 minutes(sprinkle salt liberally over the top of eggs) helps to remove shell later
  3. Nice onion
  4. Run cold water over potatoes and eggs to cool rapidly.
  5. Peel potatoes and chop, peel eggs and chop. Add together with onion, oil, vinegar, salt and pepper.
  6. Add more oil if needed. Potatoes will soak it up.
