The ingredients needed to make Tempeh with flour batter (TEMPE KEMUL):
  1. Make ready 300 gr tempe (1 stick), cut to taste with a thickness of ½ cm
  2. Prepare Basil leaves
  3. Prepare Cayenne peppers
  4. Get Oil for frying
  5. Prepare BATTER:
  6. Prepare 200 gr flour
  7. Make ready 50 gr rice flour
  8. Take 2 leeks (or 7 stalks of chives), thinly sliced/short
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp chicken flavoring
  10. Get 1/4 tsp ground pepper
  11. Get 175 ml thick coconut milk
  12. Make ready GROUND SPICES:
  13. Prepare 4 cloves garlic
  14. Prepare 8 shallots
  15. Get 1 cm kencur
  16. Take 1 section turmeric
  17. Prepare 3 pcs candlenut
  18. Take 2 tsp coriander
  19. Make ready 1 tsp salt (to taste)

Steps to make Tempeh with flour batter (TEMPE KEMUL):
  1. Mix the ground spices and batter ingredients up evenly, add a little water if necessary. Try not to let the flour clump.
  2. Heat the oil. Dip tempeh in batter, and fry over medium heat.
  3. Once the color is golden yellow and cooked, remove tempeh and drain.
  4. Serve tempeh with cayenne peppers and basil.
