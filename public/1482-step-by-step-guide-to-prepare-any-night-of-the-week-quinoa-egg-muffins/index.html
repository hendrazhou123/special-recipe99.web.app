The ingredients needed to make Quinoa egg muffins:
  1. Make ready 3/4 cup quinoa, cooked
  2. Take 1/2 cup spinach
  3. Prepare 1/2 onion, chopped
  4. Get 2 eggs
  5. Get 1/4 cup grated cheese
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp oregano
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  8. Get 1/2 tsp salt

Steps to make Quinoa egg muffins:
  1. Cook onion for 5 minutes until translucent, add spinach and cook it for another few minutes until the spinach has barely witted.
  2. In a big bowl, add cooked quinoa, cooked spinach and onion, eggs, cheese, oregano or thyme, garlic powder, salt and pepper and mix to combine.
  3. Preheat the oven at 350°F and grease the muffin pans. Pour the mixture and fill half of the muffin tins. Place the pan in oven and bake for 20 minutes.
