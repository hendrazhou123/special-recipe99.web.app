The ingredients needed to make Ragi peanut balls:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cup ragi flour
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup wheat flour
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup jaggery
  4. Take 1 cup dates
  5. Get 1 cup peanuts
  6. Prepare 2-3 cardamom
  7. Make ready 3 tablespoon Desi ghee for binding

Instructions to make Ragi peanut balls:
  1. Firstly on low flame roast both the flours together, roast very nicely because this is the main step
  2. Roast till it changes colour and nice aroma comes
  3. Roast peanuts and coarsely grind with cardamom
  4. Add jaggery and dates and grind again
  5. Mix all this in flour and add Desi ghee as required I took 3 tablespoon here
  6. Give your desired shape and store in an air tight box
