Instructions to make Butter gravy smothered sirloin with mashed carrots:
  1. Melt butter chop the onions coarsely. Add to butter.
  2. Slightly caramelize
  3. Add steak salt and pepper the steak
  4. When the steak has browned add, garlic, flour, paprika, salt and pepper
  5. When the flour has been incorporated add water till your desired thickness
  6. Stir and let the gravy simmer with the sirloin
