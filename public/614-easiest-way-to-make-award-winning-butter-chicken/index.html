The ingredients needed to make Butter Chicken:
  1. Make ready 300 g chicken Breast (Boneless)
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp Unsalted Butter
  3. Prepare 1 Red Chilli Pepper - Mince
  4. Make ready 3 Bird's Eye Chilli - Mince
  5. Make ready 10 Curry Leaves
  6. Prepare 2 Pieces Chesdale Cheese
  7. Take 200 ml Evaporated Milk
  8. Take 1 tsp Black Pepper Powder
  9. Make ready Sugar & Salt for taste
  10. Get 2 tbsp Curry Powder
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp All Purpose Flour
  12. Make ready 1 tbsp Parmesan Cheese
  13. Make ready 1 Egg

Instructions to make Butter Chicken:
  1. For chicken marinade, mixed together Sugar, Salt, Curry Powder, All Purpose Flour, Parmesan Cheese and egg into bowl.
  2. Pour marinade over chicken. Cover it and put in the refrigerator for about 1 hour.
  3. Fry chicken until it turn golden. Drain the excess oil.
  4. Heat up the pan with butter. Add red chilli pepper, birds eye chili and curry leaves. Stir until it smell good.
  5. Add evaporated milk stir until it boil. Add cheese and stir until it melt.
  6. Add black pepper powder and sugar for taste.
  7. Add the fried chicken. Stir till it blend together. Ready to serve.
