Steps to make Chocolate Banana cake:
  1. Preheat oven 350 degree Fahrenheit or heavy kadai for 15 mins with low flame
  2. A large bowl take a banana and mash it well and add palm sugar mix it well until sugar dissolved
  3. In this add milk and oil mix it well
  4. Place sieve over a bowl add all dry items and sift it flour and gently mix everything well
  5. Grease the cake pan with ghee and pour the batter in pan and put oven for 25 mins or kadai for 35 to 40 mins
  6. Then our cake ready
