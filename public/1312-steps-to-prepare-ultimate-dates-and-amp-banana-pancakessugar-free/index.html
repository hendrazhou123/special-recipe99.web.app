The ingredients needed to make Dates & banana pancakes(Sugar free):
  1. Take 1 bowl wheat flour
  2. Get As per taste Salt

Steps to make Dates & banana pancakes(Sugar free):
  1. Take a mixture jar and add dates, banana, coffee, milk, kitkat (or any chocolate) and mix it well.
  2. After that take a bowl and add wheatflour, salt, baking powder, baking soda,butter and cocoa powder.
  3. And add the paste of mixture jar. Sprinkle some choco chips.
  4. Keep the batter aside for 30 minutes, After 30 minutes. Take a pan spread butter and spread the batter in round shape.
  5. And roast it, put 4-5 pancakes in one upon another.
  6. And sprinkle choco chips and honey and here it's ready. Enjoy.
