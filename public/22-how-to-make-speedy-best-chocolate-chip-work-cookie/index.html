  2. Make ready 4 cups brown sugar
  3. Prepare 4 cups white sugar
  4. Take 1/3 cup fake vanilla extract
  5. Prepare 1 cup vegetable oil
  6. Take 9 eggs
  7. Take 2 tsp salt
  8. Make ready 4 heaping tsp baking powder
  9. Take 4 cups chocolate chips

Steps to make Best chocolate chip work cookie:
  1. Cream together butter and sugar until thoroughly mixed
  2. Add everything else BUT flour and chocolate chips. Whisk the bejeesus outta that mix.
  3. Stir in chocolate chips.
  4. Add flour and stir just until mixed.
  5. Make dough balls by using level blue (2oz) scoop. Break each ball in half. Do not flatten.
