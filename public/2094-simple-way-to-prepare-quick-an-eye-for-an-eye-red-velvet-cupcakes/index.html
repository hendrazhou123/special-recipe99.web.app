The ingredients needed to make an eye for an eye red velvet cupcakes:
  1. Get 1 1/3 cup flour
  2. Get 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  4. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  5. Get 3/4 cup unsalted butter
  6. Make ready 2/3 cup sugar
  7. Take 1 tbsp brown sugar
  8. Take 2 eggs
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp red food colouring
  10. Prepare 2 tsp vanilla extract
  11. Take 1/2 cup milk
  12. Get 1 tsp vinegar
  13. Prepare 1 frosting

Steps to make an eye for an eye red velvet cupcakes:
  1. preheat oven to 350℉
  2. sift and mix all dry ingredients, set aside
  3. cream butter and the sugars till it's light and fluffy
  4. add eggs, vanilla and red food colouring to butter mixture and mix till it's smooth
  5. add vinegar to milk, stir
  6. 3 parts dry ingredients, 2 parts milk mixture, alternatively add into the wet ingredients
  7. scoop and bake @ 350℉ for 18-20 mins, check @ 15
  8. let cool completely
  9. frost by holding piping bag straight on to achieve the eye look, decor with cutout fondant, enjoy the BOO!
