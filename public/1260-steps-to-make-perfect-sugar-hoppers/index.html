Instructions to make Sugar Hoppers:
  1. cream together butter and sugar in large bowl
  2. add egg mix well
  3. mix dry ingredients together with butter mixture
  4. add cocoa mix well
  5. gradually add milk
  6. roll on floured surface to 1/8 inch thick
  7. cut with cookie cutter
  8. bake at 350°F for 15 minutes
  9. for ease in rolling and removing cut outs, chill dough for an hour.
