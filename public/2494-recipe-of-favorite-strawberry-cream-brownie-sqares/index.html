Instructions to make Strawberry Cream Brownie Sqares:
  1. Have brownies baked and cooled
  2. Make Strawberrie Simple Syrup
  3. Combine strawberries, salt, water and sugar in a saucepan, bring to a boil then reduce heat to a simmer and cook 10 to 15 minutes, stirring often
  4. Strain through a fine mesh strainer, discard solids and place strawberry syrup in a container, cool and refrigerate. This can be made 4 days ahead if needed
  5. Make Macerated Strawberries
  6. Combine strawberries. Sliced with sugar and set aside
  7. Make Strawberry Cream
  8. Heat the 4 tabkesooons strawberry syrup and dissolve jello granules in it
  9. Whip cream to firm peaks, refrigerate while making marscapone cream
  10. Beat marscapone, strawberry simple syrup, confectioners sugar and vanilla until smooth
  11. Fold in whipped cream
  12. Assemble Molds
  13. Cut brownie using mold and fit in to be the bottom
  14. Add a layer of macerated berries
  15. Add strawberry cream almost to the to[.
