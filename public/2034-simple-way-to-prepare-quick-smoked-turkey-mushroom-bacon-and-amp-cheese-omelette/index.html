  3. Make ready 3 strips bacon cut in half
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup diced mushrooms fresh or canned

  6. Make ready Small sprinkle of shredded mozzarella cheese
  7. Make ready Small sprinkle cup shredded Colby jack cheese
