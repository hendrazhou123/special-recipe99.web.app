The ingredients needed to make Vanilla Microwave Cake:
  1. Make ready 2 tbsp Melted butter
  2. Get 1 large Egg
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp Milk
  4. Get 1/2 tsp Vanilla extract
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp Sugar
  6. Prepare 6 tbsp Self raising flour
  7. Get 1 pinch Salt
  8. Take For decoration
  9. Make ready Strawberries
  10. Take Grated coconut
  11. Prepare Left over custard
  12. Get Or anything else that you wish to use for decoration

Instructions to make Vanilla Microwave Cake:
  1. Start by mixing melted butter and the egg
  2. Add in the milk and vanilla and mix
  3. Add in the vanilla and sugar
  4. As well as the flour and pinch of salt
  5. Mix very well and place in the microwave for about 1 min 30 secs
  6. Get out on a plate and leave to cool for a couple of mins
  7. Make it look neater by cutting off the top and half it
  8. Cut the strawberries and layer them in between the cake and sandwich the two halves together
  9. Add the custard from the top and allow it to run down the sides
  10. Sprinkle with coconut and add your finishing touches to it and there you have it!!
