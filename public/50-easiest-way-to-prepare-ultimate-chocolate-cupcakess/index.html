Steps to make Chocolate Cupcakess🧁🥰:
  1. Beat eggs, and add sugar, oil, milk, vanilla essence and then beat well
  2. Sieve baking powder and flour and then add to the beated mixture and beat well
  3. Now take a cupcake tray and put cupcakes batter
  4. Bake at 150°for 20 min one by one and your cupcakes are ready
  5. If you want a thick frosting so grab a pan put oil, sugar baking powder and juat mix that
  6. Then let it cool and add fresh cream and all the type of chocolates
  7. Mix that and your frosting is ready
  8. And now do frosting and put in a air conditioned container to store for a long time
