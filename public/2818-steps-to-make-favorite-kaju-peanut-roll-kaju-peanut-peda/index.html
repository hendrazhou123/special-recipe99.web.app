Instructions to make Kaju Peanut Roll-Kaju peanut peda:
  1. Put cashew and peanuts together in a mixer jar.
  2. Grind to make a fine powder
  3. Add sugar and water to a pan… make chasni (syrup) of one string.
  4. Add cashew powder to sugar syrup and mix well then add milk powder and mix & cook till it leaves the kadhai.
  5. Let it cool… at this stage you can make peda… grease your palm with ghee and make rounds
  6. OR… flatten this with rolling pin… spread gulkand and make a roll
  7. Cut into small pieces and garnish.
