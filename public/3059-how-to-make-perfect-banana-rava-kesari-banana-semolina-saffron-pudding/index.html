The ingredients needed to make Banana Rava Kesari | Banana Semolina Saffron Pudding:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 Cup Semolina
  2. Get 2 Ripe Bananas (Mashed)
  3. Prepare 1 Cup Sugar
  4. Get 1/2 Cup Pure Ghee/Clarified Butter
  5. Make ready 4 1/2 Cup Water
  6. Prepare 10 Saffron strands
  7. Take 1/2 Tsp Yellow color
  8. Make ready 10 Cashews (Chopped & Halved)
  9. Prepare 10 Raisins

Steps to make Banana Rava Kesari | Banana Semolina Saffron Pudding:
  1. Mash the Banana with the help of a fork or potato masher. Set aside.
  2. Heat 4.5 cups of water along with the sugar & the Saffron until the sugar dissolves. Set aside.
  3. Place the Semolina along with the Pure Ghee, mashed Banana, Cashew & Raisins in a large nonstick skillet over low heat. Let it roast & sizzle. Keep stirring continuously. - Cook until light golden & aromat. Do not let it turn brown.
  4. Pour the water carefully as there might be splatters. Add the Yellow colour. Keep stirring continuously just until well incorporated. Turn off heat. - Transfer to a serving bowl. I used a food ring for plating.
  5. Garnish with halved Cashews. - Serve warm. Enjoy!
