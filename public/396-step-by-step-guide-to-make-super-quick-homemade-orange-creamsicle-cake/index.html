Instructions to make Orange Creamsicle Cake:
  1. Preheat oven 350 spray and line pan
  2. I used small 7 inch pans. Please double recipe for larger pans
  3. Whisk your dry ingredients set aside
  4. In a mixer mix sugar butter zest until fluffy and combined
  5. Add eggs 1 at a time then add vanilla
  6. Now add buttermilk and flour alternating a little at a time. Ending with flour mix until just combined
  7. Add to 2 separate pans divide evenly and bake 23-25 mins.
  8. Combine frosting ingredients using room temp butter, be sure to frost a cool cake and enjoy cake at Room temperature
