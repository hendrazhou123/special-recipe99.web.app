The ingredients needed to make Pink velvet:
  1. Get 2 cups flour (250gms)
  2. Get 1 cup sugar (200gms)
  3. Get 2 tsp baking powder
  4. Get Pinch salt
  5. Prepare 2/3 cup buttermilk (161ml)
  6. Prepare 6 tbs butter (85gms)
  7. Get 1/2 tsp almond extract
  8. Take 4 egg whites (120gms)
  9. Prepare 4 drops light red food coloring or pink color

Instructions to make Pink velvet:
  1. Whisk egg whites with sugar until fluffy,
  2. Add the rest of the liquid ingredients mix
  3. Add the dry ingredients slowly until incorporated
  4. Once mixed place in a baking tin and bake @180 for 40mins
