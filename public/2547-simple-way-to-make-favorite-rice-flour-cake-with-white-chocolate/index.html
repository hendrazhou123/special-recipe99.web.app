The ingredients needed to make Rice flour cake with white chocolate:
  1. Prepare 100 g White chocolate containing sugar
  2. Get 80 gYoghurt
  3. Take 1 Tbsp Sugar
  4. Prepare 2 Eggs
  5. Prepare 60 g Joushin-ko (Rice flour)
  6. Prepare 30 g Sugar
  7. Take 1/2 tsp Matcha Powder
  8. Make ready 1 tsp Hot water
  9. Make ready Food coloring (red) and water

Instructions to make Rice flour cake with white chocolate:
  1. Ingredients
  2. Dissolve Matcha in hot water & dissolve red food coloring in water. Separate eggs into yolks and whites.
  3. Prepare the mold. Cover inside of the mold with a parchment sheet.
  4. Melt the chocolate in a double boiler. Add the yoghurt and mix them well. When the chocolate melted completerly, take it off the heat. Add 1Tbsp sugar and mix them well.
  5. Add the yolks and mix them. Put the rice flour and mix them.
  6. Divide it into 3.
  7. Put the Matcha into 1/3 dough and mix them. Put red food coloring into another 1/3 dough and mix them.
  8. Make meringue with white of the two eggs and the 30g sugar.
  9. Mix the meringue and the 3 different color doughs. Put them into a mold. First, mix 1/3 of the meringue and the green Match dough. Put it into a mold. Smooth the surface.
  10. Mix another 1/3 of the meringue and the plain dough. Put it on the green dough in the mold. Smooth the surface.
  11. Mix the last 1/3 of the meringue and the pink dough. Put it on the plain dough in the mold. Smooth the surface.
  12. Put the mold in a steamer. (Place it on chopsticks to make a space under it.)
  13. Steam it for 20minuites over a high heat. *Water in the steamer should be boiled when you put the mold. *Put a cloth under the lid to avoid dews dropping on the cake.
  14. Unmold and put the cake on something like a toasting net to cool it. During cooling it, cover it with a cloth to keep it moisture.
