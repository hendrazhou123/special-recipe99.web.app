The ingredients needed to make Rosemary & Brown Butter Carrots:

  2. Get 3 T garlic butter
  3. Make ready 1 T rosemary
  4. Take 1/3 C honey

Steps to make Rosemary & Brown Butter Carrots:
  1. Cover carrots with cold water. Add a large pinch of sugar. Bring to a boil. Cook for approximately 3-5 minutes or until carrots are tender, but not overcooked and mushy.
  2. Drain carrots.
  3. Slowly melt garlic butter over medium-low heat until browned.
