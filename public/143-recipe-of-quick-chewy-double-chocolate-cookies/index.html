The ingredients needed to make Chewy Double Chocolate Cookies:
  1. Take 1 3/4 margarine
  2. Prepare 2 cups sugar
  3. Get 1/4 cup brown sugar
  4. Get 4 eggs
  5. Prepare 3 teaspoons vanilla
  6. Make ready 1 pkg chocolate cake mix
  7. Take 2 cups flour
  8. Take 3/4 cup cacoa
  9. Take 1 tsp salt
  10. Get 2 Cups chocolate chips
  11. Get 1 1/2 cups pecans

Instructions to make Chewy Double Chocolate Cookies:
  1. Cream butter and sugar together
  2. Add eggs and vanilla and beat well.
  3. Combine cake mix flour, cacoa baking soda and salt.
  4. Blend into creamed mixture.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips and pecans.
  6. Drops by teaspoon full into ungreased cookie sheet.
  7. Bake at 350; f for 10 min.
