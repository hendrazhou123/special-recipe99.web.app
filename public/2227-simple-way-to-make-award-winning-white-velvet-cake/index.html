The ingredients needed to make White Velvet Cake:
  1. Prepare 340 grams Cake flour sifted
  2. Take 340 grams granulated sugar
  3. Make ready 5 egg whites
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  5. Make ready 170 grams soft unsalted butter
  6. Make ready 300 ml buttermilk
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  8. Get 1 tbsp baking powder
  9. Get 120 ml oil
  10. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

Instructions to make White Velvet Cake:
  1. All the ingredients mentioned in the recipe should be at room temperature.
  2. Preheat oven to 170, line two 8 inch cake pans.
  3. In a bowl, mix flour baking powder, baking soda, salt and sugar.
  4. Divide buttermilk into 2 parts. In one, add oil and whisk well. Whisk remaining buttermilk with egg whites, vanilla extract and keep it aside
  5. Add soft butter to flour mix and blend until mixture resembles coarse sand
  6. Now add buttermilk oil mixture and blend well for 3 minutes.
  7. Now gradually add egg whites mixture and blend well.
  8. Divide batter equally and pour into prepared pans.
  9. Bake for 25-35 minutes until done.
  10. Cool for 20 minutes. Demould and cool completely.
  11. Now do whipped cream or Cream cheese frosting
  12. Slice and serve this velvety soft delicious cake
