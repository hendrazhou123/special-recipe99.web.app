The ingredients needed to make Sugar free carrot dessert:
  1. Make ready 5 carrots crumbled thinly
  2. Get 1 cup soaked almonds or any nuts
  3. Prepare Half kg milk
  4. Make ready 1 cup desi ghee
  5. Prepare 1 can condensed milk

Steps to make Sugar free carrot dessert:
  1. First use heavy base nonstick deep pan
  2. Now add crushed carrots and soak the water
  3. After look only dry immediately add ghee and roast it
  4. Now when it start leaving ghee add warm milk and start cooking at medium flame
  5. Now when carrots look cooked and softened with shiny texture pls add nuts and condensed milk
  6. Again cook until it look so crunchy and mixed will
  7. Yummy delicious sugar free dessert is ready
