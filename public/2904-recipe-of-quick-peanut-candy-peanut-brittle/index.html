Instructions to make Peanut candy (peanut brittle):
  1. In a pot add the sugar, then 1 cup water, stir until dissolved. Cover & let sugar cook
  2. Open and stir the sugar, it should have become stick after 10-15m of cooking, once it starts turning Amber, like caramel color, stir then add the peanuts
  3. Layer a parchment paper, & arrange the sticks, pour the candied peanut onto the parchment paper & stick.
  4. Allow to cool before removing from the paper. I garnished with some pistachio nuts
  5. Voilà!
