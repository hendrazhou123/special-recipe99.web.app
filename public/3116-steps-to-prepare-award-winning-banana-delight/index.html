The ingredients needed to make Banana delight:
  1. Get 3 small bananas
  2. Make ready 100 gm grated coconut
  3. Get 3 tsp sugar
  4. Make ready 4-5 cashews
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp ghee
  6. Prepare 1 pinch cardamom powder

Steps to make Banana delight:
  1. Peel the bananas and cut them in slices. In a pan, add ghee and fry the cashews in it and remove in bowl.
  2. In same pan, put banana slices and fry for 2-3 minutes. Add grated coconut and mix well.
  3. Add sugar and cardamom powder in pan and mix thoroughly. Slightly mash bananas and continuously stir the mixture till it forms like a dough. Add fried cashews in mixture.Banana delight is ready to serve with chapati, paratha or you can eat as it is.
