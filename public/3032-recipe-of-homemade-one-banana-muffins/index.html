The ingredients needed to make One Banana Muffins:
  1. Take 1 large overripe banana
  2. Make ready 1 egg white
  3. Prepare 5 tsp vegetable oil
  4. Get 1/4 cup sugar
  5. Get 1/2 cup flour
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking powder
  7. Take 1/4 tsp baking soda
  8. Get 1/8 tsp salt
  9. Get 1/8 tsp cinnamon
  10. Make ready 1 pinch nutmeg

Instructions to make One Banana Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 350F and set up a muffin pan with 4 muffin cup liners.
  2. Mash banana in medium bowl, then add all ingredients and mix well until combined.
  3. Evenly divide batter into 4 muffin cups, then bake for 20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
