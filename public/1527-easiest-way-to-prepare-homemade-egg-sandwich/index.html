  1. Take 2 bread slices
  2. Make ready 1 egg
  3. Make ready 1/2 small onion chopped

  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  7. Get 1/8 tsp red chilli powder
  8. Make ready 1/8 tsp black pepper powder
  9. Make ready 1 cheese slice
  10. Take 1 tsp butter
