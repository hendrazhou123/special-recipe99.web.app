The ingredients needed to make Microwave Red-Velvet Cake in 5 minutes!:
  1. Prepare red velvet cake
  2. Get 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  3. Make ready 1 1/2 cups sugar
  4. Prepare 3 tablespoons cocoa powder, sifted
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 teaspoons baking SODA
  6. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  7. Prepare 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  8. Get 1 cups vegetable oil
  9. Get 2 tablespoons red food coloring
  10. Take 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  11. Get cream cheese frosting
  12. Prepare 1 ltr milk (full cream for best results)
  13. Take 3 tablespoons lemon juice / vinegar
  14. Make ready 1 cup (or according to taste) granulated sugar, sifted for best results
  15. Make ready 1/2 cup butter, at room temperature
  16. Prepare 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Instructions to make Microwave Red-Velvet Cake in 5 minutes!:
  1. Sift and mix the dry ingredients together
  2. Add the wet ingredients. Mix with a whisk. Using an electric mixer will overmix the baking soda resulting to a bitter taste.
  3. Our in a microwave friendly dish and cook it for 5 minutes. * timing might vary according to your microwave.*
  4. For the cream cheese, simmer the milk in medium to low heat.
  5. Gradually add the lemon juice and stir occasionally.
  6. Let is drain out through a cheesecloth and cool down for 10 minutes.
  7. After it’s cooled down, mix the sugar, butter and vanilla extract
  8. You can scoop it on the cake or put it in a piping back
  9. Enjoy
