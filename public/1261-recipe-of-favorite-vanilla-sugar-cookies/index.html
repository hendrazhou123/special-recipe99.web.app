The ingredients needed to make Vanilla sugar cookies:
  1. Get 1 cup butter
  2. Take 1 cup sugar
  3. Get 2 eggs
  4. Get 1/2 teaspoon vanilla essence
  5. Get 1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
  6. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  8. Take 1/4 tsp bicarbonate soda

Instructions to make Vanilla sugar cookies:
  1. Pre heat oven to 180 degrees Celsius and line baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. In a large bowl cream together the butter and sugar until well combined
  3. Add the eggs and keep mixing
  4. Add vanilla essence mix untill everything is incorporated
  5. Add all the dry ingredients and mix in to make crumbly dough
  6. Cut the dough and add desired colour and knead with your finger tips to mix in the colour
  7. Cover in cling and let it rest for 10mins
  8. Roll it and cut into desired shape place on baking sheet and bake for 20mins
  9. Once ready let them cool completely and enjoy with a glass of milk
