Instructions to make red velvet pineapple surprise cake:
  1. pre heat oven 250
  2. prepared cake mix as directed
  3. greace bottom of cake pan with less than half the butter required
  4. add pineapple slices after pan has been grease
  5. add food colouring to cake mix and stir
  6. in small sauce pan add juice, remaining butter, almond extract, sugar, cornstarch and stir constantly under medium heat until a syrup is form
  7. while syrup is hot, pouring slowly over pineapples to fill bottom of pan
  8. add cake mixture to pan and bake as directed
