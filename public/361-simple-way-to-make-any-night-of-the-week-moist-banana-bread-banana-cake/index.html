The ingredients needed to make Moist Banana Bread 香蕉蛋糕 Banana cake:
  1. Get 5 medium size ripe bananas
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
  3. Take 1 large egg ; room temperature
  4. Take 45 g unsalted butter ; melted
  5. Get 2 cups cake flour
  6. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Make ready 1 tsb baking soda
  8. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Take Pitch of ground cardamom (option)
  10. Make ready 40 g walnuts
  11. Prepare 40 g chocolate chips

Steps to make Moist Banana Bread 香蕉蛋糕 Banana cake:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350F (180C)
  2. Pour wet ingredients to mashed banana and stir to combine
  3. In a big bowl, smash bananas
  4. In another bowl, combine sugar, egg, melted butter and stir well
  5. Sift cake flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt and cardamom into the wet ingredients, and split two times.
  6. Add walnuts and chocolate chips. Mix well but do not over mix
  7. Pour the batter into the 9"x5" loaf pan
  8. Bake for 45 to 55 minutes, or until you inserted a wooden pick in center of cake comes out clean
