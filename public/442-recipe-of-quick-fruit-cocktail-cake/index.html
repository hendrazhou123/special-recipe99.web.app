Steps to make Fruit Cocktail Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Spray 9x13 pan.
  2. Mix first 5 ingredients until well blended
  3. Pour into pan and bake 30-35 minutes.
  4. Poke a few holes in cake with a fork.
  5. Combine remaining ingredients in a medium sauce pan and bring to a boil for 1 minute.
  6. Pour warm sauce over warm cake, spread coconut and pecans evenly.
