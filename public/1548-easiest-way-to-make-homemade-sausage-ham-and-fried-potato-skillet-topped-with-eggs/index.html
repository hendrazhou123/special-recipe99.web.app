The ingredients needed to make Sausage, ham and fried potato skillet topped with eggs:
  1. Make ready 5-6 potatoes, washed ends cut off
  2. Take 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  3. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  4. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  5. Take Dash seasoning salt and pepper
  6. Take Parsley flakes to garnish
  7. Make ready 1 cup sausage
  8. Make ready 3 slices deli ham
  9. Prepare 2 eggs
  10. Make ready 1 piece bread
  11. Take 1/4 cup shredded Colby jack cheese
  12. Get Oil for frying

Instructions to make Sausage, ham and fried potato skillet topped with eggs:
  1. Heat oil in a skillet and dice potatoes to desired size. The thinner they are sliced the fast they will cook. Stir in all dry seasonings except parsley and cook potatoes uncovered on low about 20 minutes until cooked through and crispy on the outside.
  2. Make a well in the pan and place sausage in the center and break up as it cooks using spatula. Cook until fully brown about 5 minutes.
  3. Dice ham or any deli meat you prefer and stir in and cook 2 more minutes.
  4. Place on plate and make your two eggs in a separate pan. I like over medium eggs but if you want scrambled in your skillet you can put them in the well and cook with the sausage.
