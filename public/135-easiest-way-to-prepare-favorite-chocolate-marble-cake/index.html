The ingredients needed to make Chocolate marble cake:
  1. Make ready 100 g flour
  2. Prepare 100 g butter
  3. Take 90 g sugar
  4. Make ready 2 eggs
  5. Get 50 g cocoa powder
  6. Take 1 teaspoon baking powder

Steps to make Chocolate marble cake:
  1. Put butter in a bowl. Heat it on microwave for 20 seconds.
  2. Beat butter until it is soft.
  3. Add sugar and beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy in a bowl.
  4. Add eggs in a bowl and mix well.
  5. Add flour in a bowl and mix well.
  6. Take out 1/3 mixture to another bowl. Add cocoa powder in it and mix well.
  7. Mix cocoa mixture in a bowl.
  8. Pour the mixture in a cake pad. Bake it in 180C oven for 35 minutes.
