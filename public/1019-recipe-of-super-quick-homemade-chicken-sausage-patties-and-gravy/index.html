The ingredients needed to make Chicken Sausage Patties and Gravy:
  1. Prepare Sausage
  2. Take 500 g ground chicken
  3. Prepare 4 garlic cloves, chopped fine
  4. Prepare 1 tsp onion powder
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp chilli powder
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp paprika
  7. Make ready 1 tsp ground cumin seed
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground oregano
  9. Take 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  10. Prepare 1 tsp garlic salt
  11. Get 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  12. Prepare 2 tsp vegetable oil
  13. Prepare Gravy
  14. Make ready 1/4 cup shortening (Crisco)
  15. Get 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  16. Get 2 cups chicken broth
  17. Make ready 1/2 tsp paprika
  18. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper

Instructions to make Chicken Sausage Patties and Gravy:
  1. Blend the garlic and the spices into the ground chicken. Let stand in the refrigerator overnight to let the meat absorb the spices.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet under medium heat until hot but not smoking. Roll the spiced sausage meat into golf ball size rounds and flatten to around 1 cm thickness. Fry under medium heat until cooked through (75Â°C interior temperature), about six minutes per side.
  3. In the same skillet, mix shortening and flour under low heat until well combined
  4. Add water, increase heat to medium, and bring to a boil, stirring. Add salt, pepper, and paprika. Stir until the gravy has thickened, about five minutes.
  5. Serve with buttermilk biscuits or rice.
