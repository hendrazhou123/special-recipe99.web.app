  4. Get 8 Egg yokes
  5. Make ready 3/4 Cup buttermilk

  7. Make ready 2 tsp Baking powder
  8. Take 1 Jar Marciano cherries
  9. Get 2 Cups King Arthur flour
  10. Take 2 cans sliced pineapple
  11. Prepare 1 tsp Salt

Steps to make PineappleUpside down cake:
  1. Cream together Smart balance (butter) & suger.
  2. Add the salt, baking powder, vanilla mix well
  3. Add egg yolks & mix well
  4. Add flour & buttermilk mix well.
  5. Lay out pineapple, walnuts & cherry’s in one of your cake pans. (This will be your top)
  6. Lay out your bottom pan with just pineapples on the bottom & sides.
  7. Pour batter in pans evenly & bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
