The ingredients needed to make Red Velvet Whoopee Pies:
  1. Prepare Red Velvet Cake
  2. Prepare 2 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  3. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Take 1 1/2 cup sugar
  5. Take 1 tsp salt
  6. Take 2 tsp cocoa
  7. Prepare 2 cup vegetable oil
  8. Take 2 eggs
  9. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla
  10. Make ready 1 tsp vinegar
  11. Prepare 1 cup buttermilk
  12. Get 1 oz red food coloring
  13. Prepare Cream cheese icing
  14. Prepare 2 8 oz cream cheese
  15. Make ready 2 cup powdered sugar
  16. Take 1 tbsp vanilla

Instructions to make Red Velvet Whoopee Pies:
  1. Sift together flour, soda, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add cocoa.
  3. Mix vegetable oil, eggs, vanilla, vinegar, buttermilk, and food coloring
  4. Add flour mixture to liquid mixture.
  5. Blend well
  6. Drop my tablespoon into a greased baking sheet.
  7. Bake at 350° for 8-10 minutes
  8. Allow to cool fully
  9. Place one tablespoon of cream cheese icing between two red velvet cookies
  10. Smash together.
  11. Enjoy!
