The ingredients needed to make Light (low sugar) Ice Cream:

Instructions to make Light (low sugar) Ice Cream:
  1. Whip 3 of the 4 cups of heavy cream.
  2. Mix in remaining ingredients and 4th cup of heavy cream.
  3. Scoop into resealable bowl and put in freezer.
  4. Stir after 1 hour and again after 2 hours, then freeze overnight.
