Instructions to make Homemade Egg Noodles:
  1. Mix salt and flour in a large mixing bowl. Make a small well in the mixture with your fist add your eggs in well. Mix with your hands.
  2. Add in about 1 tsp of water at a time until you can knead the dough.
  3. Separate dough in fourths. Roll out on a well floured surface to desired thickness and use a pizza cutter to cut noodle strips.
  4. Cook noodles in boiling water for about 12 minutes or until cooked through and then drain.
