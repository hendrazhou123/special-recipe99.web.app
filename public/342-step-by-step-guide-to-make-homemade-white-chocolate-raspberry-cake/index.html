The ingredients needed to make White Chocolate Raspberry Cake:
  1. Prepare 150 g White Chocolate
  2. Take 75 g Butter
  3. Get 3 Eggs
  4. Make ready 2 Tsp Baking Powder
  5. Make ready 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  6. Get 2 Tbsp Milk (Optional)
  7. Prepare 150 g Sugar
  8. Take 300 g All-purpose Flour
  9. Take 45 g Almond/Chopped Walnuts
  10. Get 200 g Frozen Raspberries
  11. Get Pinch Of Salt
