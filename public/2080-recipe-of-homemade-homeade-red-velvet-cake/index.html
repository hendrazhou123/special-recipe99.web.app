The ingredients needed to make Homeade Red Velvet Cake:
  1. Take cake
  2. Make ready 2 eggs
  3. Take 2 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  4. Make ready 1 1/2 cup Sugar
  5. Prepare 2 cup vegetable oil
  6. Make ready 3 tbsp cocoa
  7. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  8. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  9. Prepare 3 tbsp red food coloring
  10. Prepare 1 cup buttermilk
  11. Make ready 1 tsp vinegar
  12. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  13. Take icing
  14. Get 1 box 10x Powdered sugar
  15. Take 1 stick butter
  16. Take 8 oz cream cheese
  17. Make ready 1 cup chopped pecans

Steps to make Homeade Red Velvet Cake:
  1. Grease and lightly flour 2 8x2inch pans or line 2 12 cup muffin pans with cupcake liners.
  2. Preheat oven to 350°
  3. Mix food coloring and cocoa together. Add vinegar stir and set to side
  4. Sift flour,salt,and soda together and set to side.
  5. Cream Sugar and Oil. Add eggs one at a time
  6. Add food coloring mixture to egg mixture and mix well
  7. Alternately add flour mixture and butter milk to egg mixture and mix well
  8. Add vanilla extract mix well
  9. Pour bake into baking pans and bake on center rack for 25-30 minutes (20-25 for cupcakes)
  10. Allow to completely cool before icing
  11. Mix cream chesse butter and powdered sugar until smooth add pecans
