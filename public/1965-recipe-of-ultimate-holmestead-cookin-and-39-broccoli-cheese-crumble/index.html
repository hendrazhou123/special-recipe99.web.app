Instructions to make Holmestead Cookin': Broccoli Cheese Crumble:
  1. In food processor, chop broccoli into small pieces
  2. Place in microwavable bowl, and add water until all broccoli is covered. Microwave on high for 3 minutes.
  3. Put broccoli in an oven-safe tray.
  4. In a pot, add Velveeta cheese and milk, and heat on medium-low (stirring occasionally) until fully liquid.
  5. Pour cheese over broccoli.
  6. Put Ritz Crackers into food processor, and turn to dust.
  7. Cover broccoli and cheese with cracker dust.
  8. Place in oven for 30 minutes at 350F.
  9. Let cool for 5 minutes, and serve.
