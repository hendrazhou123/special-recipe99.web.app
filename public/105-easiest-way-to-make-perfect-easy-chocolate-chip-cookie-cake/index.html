The ingredients needed to make Easy Chocolate Chip cookie CAKE:
  1. Get 2 cups AP flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  3. Prepare 1 tsp bakingpowder
  4. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  5. Get 2 sticks butter
  6. Prepare 1 egg
  7. Prepare 1.5 tsp vanilla
  8. Take 1 cup brown sugar
  9. Prepare 1 cup white sugar
  10. Take 2 cups chocolate chips
  11. Prepare Optional Frosting
  12. Get 1 stick butter
  13. Take 1 tsp vanilla
  14. Prepare 1.5 cups confectioners sugar (add more or less to your liking)
  15. Prepare 1-2 tbs milk, heavy cream or plant milk

Instructions to make Easy Chocolate Chip cookie CAKE:
  1. Preheat oven 350 F
  2. Whisk dry ingredients and set aside. Prepare frosting by combining those ingredients, then add to piping bag or ziplock and set aside
  3. Cream together butter and sugar until smooth
  4. Add egg and vanilla combine well
  5. Slowly add flour and then fold in chips
  6. Get a 9 inch baking pan and spray with oil, after flatten cookie dough down to the edges of pan. Bake 20-25 min. Check and rotate pan after 15 min
