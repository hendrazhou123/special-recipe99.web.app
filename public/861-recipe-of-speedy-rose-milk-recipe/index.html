Steps to make Rose milk recipe:
  1. Step 1) Take the grated beetroot and squeeze the juice into a bowl and keep aside.
  2. Step 2) In a heavy bottomed vessel, add sugar and water dissolve - well and bring this to a boil.
  3. Step 3) Now add the beetroot juice and allow it to boil till it forms a - sticky consistency (a small string forms and breaks).
  4. Step 4) Switch off the flame and add the rose water.
  5. Step 5) Cool down completely then pour into a clean dry glass bottle, - and store in the fridge.
  6. Step 6) Add 2-3 tsp of this rose syrup in a glass.
  7. Step 7) Add cold milk and mix well and enjoy the delicious rose milk.
