The ingredients needed to make Peanut Makhana chaat:
  1. Get 1 cup peanuts
  2. Take 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  3. Prepare 1 boiled potato
  4. Take 1 small tomato
  5. Prepare 1-2 green chillies chopped
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp black pepper
  7. Make ready As required Grated fresh coconut optional
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup makahna /foxnuts
  9. Take Handful Chopped coriander
  10. Prepare As per taste Green chutney
  11. Make ready to taste Lemon juice
  12. Get to taste Salt
  13. Take 2 tsp desi ghee

Steps to make Peanut Makhana chaat:
  1. Soak the peanuts about for 4-5 hours then boil these in the cooker for 1 whistle. Then let it cool and take out on a plate.
  2. Now Heat ghee in a pan,add foxnuts and roast until it becomes crispy then take out in a bowl. Cut the potato in small pieces.
  3. Now add remaining ghee in the same pan,add cumin seeds, chopped green chillies. Roast it. Then add boiled peanuts. Mix it well and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add the pieces of potato, salt, black pepper to it and mix it well. Roast for 2 minutes then add grated coconut. (Optional) and roasted foxnuts. Mix all ingredients well then Switch off the flame.
  5. Serve this chaat in a bowl or plate. Garnish with chopped tomato, coriander leaves, lemon juice and green chutney.
