The ingredients needed to make Keto coconut flour cheese pandesal:
  1. Take 3 cups coconut flour
  2. Prepare 1.5 cup Psyllium powder
  3. Make ready 4 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 2 tsp sea salt
  5. Take 1 tbsp sweetener
  6. Prepare 4 tsp apple cider
  7. Get 3 egg yolks
  8. Make ready 80 grams butter
  9. Get 4 cups water
  10. Get 3 egg whites
  11. Make ready 1/2 tsp cream of tartar
  12. Get 1/2 cup cubes cheese

Steps to make Keto coconut flour cheese pandesal:
  1. Mix all ingredients except egg white and tartar
  2. Beat egg white and cream of tartar until frothy, then add to the the flour mixture
  3. Preheat oven at 170 degree celcius
  4. Add cubes cheese to the prepared dough ball
  5. Bake the dough at 1 hour and 15 mins
