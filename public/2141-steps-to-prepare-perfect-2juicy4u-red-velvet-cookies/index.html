The ingredients needed to make 2juicy4u Red Velvet Cookies:
  1. Get 1 box Duncan Red Velvet cake mix
  2. Prepare 2 each eggs
  3. Get 1 1/2 tbsp vanila extact
  4. Get 6 oz White chocolate chips
  5. Get 16 oz Betty Crocker cream cheese icing
  6. Get 1/2 cup Butter (softened)

Steps to make 2juicy4u Red Velvet Cookies:
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350.
  2. Mix half of the cake mix in a bowl with the eggs, butter & vanilla.
  3. Mix until smooth.
  4. Add the other half of cake mix & white choc. chips. Mix well.
  5. Using an un-greased cookie sheet & ice cream scooper place scoops of cookie mix onto the cookie sheet. Place scoops at least 2 inches a part.
  6. Place in the oven for 12-15 minutes.
  7. Remove from the oven when done & allow to cool completely.
  8. After cookies have cooled, heavily spread icing on top.
  9. Enjoy!
