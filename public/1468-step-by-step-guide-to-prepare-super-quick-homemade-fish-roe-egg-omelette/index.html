Steps to make Fish roe (egg) omelette:
  1. Boil fish roe with turmeric powder, chilli powder and little salt with 1cup of water, 2tbs oil and cover and cook in medium flame until water is fully observe. Let it cool
  2. Beat eggs, add all the chopped Ingredients, and rosemary. Add salt to taste
  3. Mash fish egg and add with egg mix and combine throughly
  4. Heat a pan and pour 3tbs oil, spread the mix, cover and cook in medium low flame for 8 min, then cut into four pieces, turn and cook for another 8 to 10 min
  5. Serve hot with butter cheese dip
