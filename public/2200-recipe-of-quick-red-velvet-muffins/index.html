The ingredients needed to make Red Velvet Muffins:
  1. Prepare 1 cup maida
  2. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup butter
  4. Take 1 tsp red edible colour
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp cinnamon powder
  6. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla essence
  8. Get 1/2 cup milk
