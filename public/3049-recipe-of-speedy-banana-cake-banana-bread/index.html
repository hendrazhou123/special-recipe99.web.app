The ingredients needed to make Banana Cake ; Banana Bread:
  1. Make ready 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder
  3. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  5. Get 1/2 cup white sugar
  6. Take 1/2 cup oil
  7. Make ready 1 cup milk
  8. Make ready 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  9. Take 2 medium bansnas - mashed
  10. Get 1 cup chocolate chips
  11. Get 1 tbsp vanilla
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  13. Prepare for topping
  14. Make ready 1/4 cup chocolate chips
  15. Get 1 Banana - sliced

Steps to make Banana Cake ; Banana Bread:
  1. Pre heat oven at 160 degrees Celsius
  2. Spray your pan with oil
  3. Combine milk and Apple vinegar,and set aside for 10 minutes
  4. Mix the dry ingredients together ; flour, backing powder, backing soda,salt, cinnamon, and sugar
  5. Combine, mashed bananas, oil & vanilla to the milk vinegar mixture, and wisk.
  6. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients, mix for 1 to 2 minutes.Don't over mix, add chocolate chips.
  7. Pour batter in the pan, sprinkle ¼ cup chocolate chips, and the sliced banana.
  8. Bake for 45 to 55 minutes, or untill toothpick comes out clean. P.S at minute 45 you can check your cake not before.
  9. Take the cake off the mold when it's completely cold
