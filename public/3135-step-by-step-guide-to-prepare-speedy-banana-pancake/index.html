Instructions to make Banana Pancake:
  1. Mash banana using fork (keep aside)
  2. Add sugar and egg. Mix with mashed banana. (Mix using spatula)
  3. Add flour, salt and vanillin. (Mix using spatula)
  4. Add milk and mix well using spatula
  5. Heat non stick pan. Spread butter to easily toss the pancake
  6. Serve with banana and honey (kids would love this)
