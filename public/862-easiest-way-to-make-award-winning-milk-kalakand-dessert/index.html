The ingredients needed to make Milk Kalakand dessert:
  1. Take 4 litres Full fat Milk
  2. Prepare 3-4 cups Sugar
  3. Make ready 2 pinch Green Cardamom powder
  4. Take 1/2 cup Desi Ghee
  5. Prepare 1 tablespoon Sooji/Rava
  6. Get Few strands of original kasmiri kesar or Saffron
