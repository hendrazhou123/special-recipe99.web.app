The ingredients needed to make Bourbon treacle and vanilla granola:
  1. Make ready bourbon canola
  2. Make ready 1 cup Bourbon
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup real maple syrup
  4. Prepare 4 tbsp real vanilla extract
  5. Prepare 3 tbsp of treacle
  6. Take 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  7. Take 1 tsp ground clove
  8. Prepare 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  9. Prepare 3 tbsp oil
  10. Take 2 tbsp honey
  11. Get 1 cup light brown sugar
  12. Make ready 1 kg rolled oats
  13. Take 250 grams mix fruit and nuts
  14. Get 50 grams seeds any
  15. Take 200 grams pecans
  16. Prepare 1 cup honey crusted cashews
