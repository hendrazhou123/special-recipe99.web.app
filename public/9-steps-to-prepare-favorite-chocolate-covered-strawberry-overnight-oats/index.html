The ingredients needed to make Chocolate covered strawberry overnight oats:
  1. Prepare 1 cup oats
  2. Prepare 1 cup almond milk or milk (I used milk)
  3. Make ready 4 tablespoons maple syrup (I used regular syrup)
  4. Get 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  5. Get 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  6. Take 2 tablespoons sugar (only if using regular syrup)
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. Take 2 tablespoon mini chocolate chips
  9. Get 1 cup sliced strawberries
