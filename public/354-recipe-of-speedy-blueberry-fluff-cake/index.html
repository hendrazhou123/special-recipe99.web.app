Instructions to make Blueberry fluff cake ðŸ¥®:
  1. Micx the sugars together
  2. Beat butter and sugars until fluffy. With an electric mixer, on medium speed, for about two minutes.
  3. Whisk two cups flour, baking powder, and salt together in a medium bowl.
