The ingredients needed to make Chicken teriyaki:
  1. Get 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  2. Make ready 1 Chicken breast
  3. Make ready 4 tbsp soy
  4. Take 3 tbsp honey
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp white sugar
  6. Get 2 tbsp white vinegar
  7. Get 1 minced garlic
  8. Get Green onion (optional)
  9. Make ready Sesame seeds (optional)
  10. Make ready Sushi rice (optional)

Steps to make Chicken teriyaki:
  1. Cook chopped chicken breast with vegetable oil in pan
  2. Mix honey, soy, sugar, vinegar, and garlic
  3. In a separate pan heat the sauce till it starts to bubble
  4. Add chicken and mix with sauce on low heat till well coated
  5. Add chopped green onion and cook for 30 seconds
  6. Plate and add sesame seeds
  7. If you please you can cook rice and serve with it
