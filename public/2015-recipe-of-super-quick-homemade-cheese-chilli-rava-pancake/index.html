The ingredients needed to make Cheese chilli rava pancake:
  1. Take 2 spoons cheese chilli left in refrigerator
  2. Make ready 2 tablespoon rava semolina
  3. Take Half teaspoon salt
  4. Take Half teaspoon chaat masala
  5. Get Half teaspoon red chilli powder
  6. Make ready Half teaspoon coriander powder
  7. Take Half teaspoon garam masala
  8. Get 1 teas rai or black mustard seeds
  9. Get 1 teaspoon cumin
  10. Prepare 2 tablespoons curd

Steps to make Cheese chilli rava pancake:
  1. First mix rava and spices and curd.mix well and left for 15 minutes.make a smooth paste
  2. Now in a pan roast in oil.cumin seeds+rai
  3. Add it to rava batter
  4. Now in a nonstick pan spread some oil and spread rava mixture.make upper layer of cheese chilli.now on very low flame or you can turn the gas off if the utensil is hot enough,cover the utensil and turn the side after 2 minutes
  5. Now again cover and check after 2 minutes if it looks like a smooth cooked pancake
  6. Enjoy with any sauce.my kids loved my all rava pancakes
