The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip,pb & banana sandwiches:
  1. Take 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  2. Take 2 tablespoons honey
  3. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  4. Prepare 2 tablespoons miniature chocolate chips
  5. Get 4 slices whole wheat bread
  6. Prepare 1 medium banana

Instructions to make Chocolate chip,pb & banana sandwiches:
  1. Mix the peanut butter,honey and cinnamon;stir in chocolate chips.
  2. Spread over bread.
  3. Layer two bread slices with banana slices;top with remaining bread.
  4. If desired,cut into shapes using cookie cutters.
