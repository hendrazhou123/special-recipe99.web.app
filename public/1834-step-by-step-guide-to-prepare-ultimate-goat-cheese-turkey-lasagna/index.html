The ingredients needed to make Goat cheese turkey lasagna:
  1. Get 1 lb ground turkey 85 / 15
  2. Take 2 (24 oz) jars rao's homemade marinara sauce
  3. Prepare 1 (6 oz) can tomato paste I use Italian style
  4. Get 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon white sugar
  6. Get 2 teaspoons dried basil
  7. Make ready 3 (4 oz) containers of goat cheese crumbles
  8. Take 1 (12 oz) container of whipped cream cheese
  9. Get 1 teaspoons course sea salt
  10. Prepare 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  11. Take 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  12. Get 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  13. Prepare 2 eggs
  14. Take 1/3 cup whole milk
  15. Prepare 12 no boil lasagna noodles
  16. Make ready 18 slices sargento mozzarella cheese
  17. Get 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese for topping
  18. Get About 2 teaspoons dried parsley for topping

Instructions to make Goat cheese turkey lasagna:
  1. In a large skillet on medium high heat cook and mince up ground turkey till done then drain. Return back to skillet and stir in garlic, basil, sugar, tomato paste and both jars of sauce and heat up till bubbling then reduce heat to low and simmer for about 20 minutes stirring occasionally.
  2. Pre heat oven to 375 degrees
  3. Mix goat cheese, whipped cream cheese, egg, grated Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, milk and parsley together
  4. Lightly spray a 10×13 ×3 inch baking dish with cooking oil I use olive oil
  5. Spread about 1 1/2 cups meat sauce in bottom
  6. Lay 4 noodles in bottom side by side
  7. Cover noodles with about 1 cup of the goat cheese mixture
  8. Add 6 cheese slices in rows
  9. Then 1 1/2 cups meat sauce repeat layers two more times ending with meat sauce
  10. Sprinkle on shredded Parmesan cheese and parsley
  11. Bake for 1 hour covered tightly with foil. I'll lightly spray the foil with cooking spray so the cheese doesn't stick
  12. Then bake for 15 more minutes with foil cover off
  13. Let rest for 10 minutes then enjoy with garlic bread and or salad.
